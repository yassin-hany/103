Northwind Electronics — Product FAQ
=====================================

Q: What products does Northwind Electronics manufacture?
A: Northwind Electronics manufactures consumer and commercial electronics
   including smart home hubs, wireless speakers, portable power stations,
   and industrial IoT sensor modules.

Q: What is the standard warranty period?
A: All Northwind Electronics products come with a 2-year limited warranty
   from the date of purchase. The warranty covers manufacturing defects
   and component failures under normal use conditions.

Q: Does the warranty cover accidental damage?
A: No. The standard warranty does not cover accidental damage, water damage,
   unauthorized modifications, or damage caused by use outside the product's
   rated specifications. Extended protection plans that include accidental
   damage coverage are available for purchase separately.

Q: How do I file a warranty claim?
A: Contact Northwind Electronics support at support@northwindelectronics.com
   with your proof of purchase and a description of the issue. A support
   representative will respond within 2 business days with a Return
   Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and shipping instructions.

Q: What is the return policy for unused products?
A: Unopened products may be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full
   refund. Opened products in original condition may be returned within
   14 days for a refund minus a 15% restocking fee.

Q: Are replacement parts available for purchase?
A: Yes. Replacement batteries, power adapters, mounting brackets, and sensor
   modules are available through the Northwind Electronics parts store.
   Parts orders typically ship within 3 business days.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: Northwind Electronics accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, bank
   transfers, and purchase orders for commercial accounts with approved
   credit terms.

Q: Does Northwind Electronics offer volume discounts?
A: Yes. Orders of 50 or more units qualify for tiered volume pricing.
   Contact the commercial sales team at sales@northwindelectronics.com
   for a custom quote.
